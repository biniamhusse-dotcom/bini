Admit Patient: Inform the liaison officer to admit the patient to the ward you selected.

Refer Patient: Inform the liaison officer to refer the patient out of UoG Hospital.

Link Patient: Send the patient to other OPD (start visit for the patient at the OPD) you selected or send the patient to other health care providers available at UoG Hospital .

Transfer Patient: Inform the liaison officer or ward Nurse to transfer the patient to other ward available at UoG Hospital.

Discharge Patient: Inform the liaison officer or ward Nurse to discharge the patient out of the ward.

Keep Patient: Select this option if the patient needs other investigation before he/she goes to home.

Leaving Against Medical Advice and Service: Select this option if the patient against any service or advice.
